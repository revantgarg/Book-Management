My Favourites

Harry Potter

Harry Potter is a series of seven fantasy novels written by British author J. K. Rowling and published from 1997 to 2007. The novels chronicle the lives of a young wizard, Harry Potter, and his friends Hermione Granger and Ron Weasley, all of whom are students at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry and an evil Wozard Lord Voldemort.
